Alloy

Added 13 new Alloy currency items, which can be unlocked through Remnant encounters after completing Farrow's quest in Act 2. These items add various new crafted modifiers to items by replacing an existing modifier, similar to Perfect Essences.

Alloys are a type of currency exclusive to Runes of Aldur league that can be used on specific classes of rare items to add a guaranteed modifier to an item.

  • Runic Alloy
  • Adaptive Alloy
  • Protective Alloy
  • Expansive Alloy
  • Swift Alloy
  • Cyclonic Alloy
  • Prismatic Alloy
  • Mystic Alloy
  • Sovereign Alloy
  • Celestial Alloy
  • Transcendent Alloy
  • The Runebinder's Alloy
  • The Runefather's Alloy
