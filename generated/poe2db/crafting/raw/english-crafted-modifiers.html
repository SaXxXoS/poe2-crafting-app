Crafted Modifiers
Some methods of item crafting guarantee that a specific Modifier will be crafted onto the item. An item can only have one Crafted Modifier, but they otherwise behave identically to other Modifiers.

Crafted Modifiers are displayed in a lighter blue colour than regular Modifiers.
