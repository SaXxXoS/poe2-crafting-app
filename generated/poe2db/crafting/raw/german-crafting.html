Mechanics

Upgrading rarity
Currency Restriction Effect
Orb of Transmutation, Greater Orb of Transmutation, Perfect Orb of Transmutation Normal Upgrades a Normal to a Magic, adding 1 affix
Orb of Alchemy Normal Upgrades a Normal to a Rare, adding 4 affixes
Orb of Chance Normal Upgrades a Normal to a Unique, or destroys it
Regal Orb, Greater Regal Orb, Perfect Regal Orb Magic Upgrade Magic to Rare, adding 1 affix.
Lesser Essence, Essence, Greater Essence Magic Upgrade Magic to Rare, adding 1 specific affix.

Omens:

  • Omen of Sinistral Alchemy: The next Orb of Alchemy inflicts the maximum number of prefixes.
  • Omen of Dextral Alchemy: The next Orb of Alchemy inflicts the maximum number of suffixes.
  • Omen of Sinistral Coronation: The next Regal Orb will only have a prefix added.
  • Omen of Dextral Coronation: The next Regal Orb will only have a prefix added.
  • Omen of Homogenising Coronation: The next Regal Orb adds an affix of the same type as the one currently on the item.
  • Omen of the Ancients: The next Orb of Chance upgrades the item to a random Unique item of the same type.
  • Omen of Chance: The next Orb of Chance does not destroy the item.
Augmenting and removing modifiers
Currency Restrictions Effects
Orb of Augmentation, Greater Orb of Augmentation, Perfect Orb of Augmentation Magic Magic Adds 1 affix to magic item.
Exalted Orb, Greater Exalted Orb, Perfect Exalted Orb Rare Adds 1 affix to rare item.
Orb of Annulment Magic or Rare Removes 1 affix from Magic or Rare item, without affecting rarity.
Chaos Orb, Greater Chaos Orb, Perfect Chaos Orb Rare Rare item remove 1 affix and add 1 affix.
Perfect Essence Rare Removes a random modifier and augments a Rare item with a new guaranteed modifier

Omens:

  • Omen of Greater Exaltation: The next Exalted Orb adds 2 affixes.
  • Omen of Sinistral Exaltation: The next Exalted Orb adds 1 prefix.
  • Omen of Dextral Exaltation: The next Exalted Orb adds 1 suffix.
  • Omen of Homogenising Exaltation: The next Exalted Orb adds an affix of the same type as the one currently on this item.
  • Omen of Greater Annulment: The next Orb of Annulment removes 2 affixes.
  • Omen of Sinistral Annulment: The next Orb of Annulment removes 1 prefix.
  • Omen of Dextral Annulment: The next Orb of Annulment removes 1 suffix.
  • Omen of Sinistral Erasure: The next Chaos Orb removes 1 prefix.
  • Omen of Dextral Erasure: The next Chaos Orb removes 1 suffix.
  • Omen of Whittling: The next Chaos Orb removes the lowest level affix.
  • Omen of Sinistral Crystallisation: Your next Perfect or Corrupted Essence removes the prefix.
  • Omen of Dextral Crystallisation: Your next Perfect or Corrupted Essence removes the suffix.
Desecration
Currency Restriction Effect
Ancient Collarbone, Preserved Collarbone, Gnawed Collarbone Rare Necklace, Ring, or Belt Desecration
Ancient Jawbone, Preserved Jawbone, Gnawed Jawbone Rare Weapon or Quiver Desecration
Ancient Rib, Preserved Rib, Gnawed Rib Rare Armor Desecration
Preserved Cranium Rare Jewel Desecration

Omens:

  • Omen of Abyssal Echoes: The next Desecration roll grants a single reroll.
  • Omen of Dextral Necromancy: The next Desecration roll grants only a suffix.
  • Omen of Sinistral Necromancy: The next Desecration roll grants only a prefix.
  • Omen of Putrefaction: The next Desecration roll will replace all affixes on the item, creating an item with up to 6 Desecrated affixes and corrupting the item.
  • Omen of the Blackblooded: The next Desecration roll will grant a random Kurgal affix to weapons and jewellery.
  • Omen of the Liege: The next Desecration roll will grant a random Amanamu affix to weapons and jewellery.
  • Omen of the Sovereign: The next Desecrate will grant a random Ulaman affix to weapons and jewellery.
  • Omen of Light: The next Orb of Annulment will only remove Desecrate affixes.
